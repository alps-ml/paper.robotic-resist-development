Gascontrol
==========

We use the following valve to connect to our nitrogen lines.
 - Beduan 2 Way Normally Closed DC 12V Electric Solenoid Air Valve 1/4" NPT - https://www.amazon.com/Beduan-Normally-Closed-Electric-Solenoid/dp/B07N2LGFYS
 - USB Serial Port Relay Module - https://www.amazon.com/JESSINIE-Lightweight-Intelligent-Overcurrent-Protection/dp/B0BXDNNJ8K

The 3d printed mount allows to screw the valve on a Thorlabs post.
